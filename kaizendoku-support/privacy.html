KaizenDoku Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 20, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how KaizenDoku collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our iOS application. By using KaizenDoku, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We minimize data collection to provide a great gaming experience. Below are the types of information that may be collected:

  • Local Game Data:
    • All game progress, puzzle history, and performance data is stored locally on your device.
    • No personal information is required to play KaizenDoku.
  • Sign in with Apple (Optional):
    • If you choose to use Sign in with Apple, we receive a unique anonymized user ID to back up and sync your progress across devices.
    • You may use Apple's private email relay — we never require your real email address.
  • Advertising Data (Kudosu Mode):
    • KaizenDoku uses Google AdMob to serve ads in Kudosu Mode.
    • On iOS 14.5 and later, KaizenDoku will request your permission to track your device for advertising purposes using Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. If you grant permission, AdMob may use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) to serve personalized ads. If you deny permission, only non-personalized ads will be served.
    • You can change your tracking preference at any time in Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.
    • AdMob's data practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Support Requests:
    • If you contact us via email, we may collect your email address and any information you provide to resolve your inquiry.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Store and display your puzzle progress and performance metrics locally on your device.
  • Optionally sync progress across devices when using Sign in with Apple.
  • Serve ads in Kudosu Mode via Google AdMob.
  • Respond to support inquiries.

We do not sell your data or use it for purposes beyond those listed above.

3. Data Sharing

  • Apple: Sign in with Apple data is managed by Apple under their Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdMob: Ad serving in Kudosu Mode is handled by Google AdMob. See Google's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle data.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

We do not share your data with any other third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All game data is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage.
  • No game data is transmitted to external servers except where you opt in to Sign in with Apple.
  • We do not operate or maintain backend servers that store your personal information.

5. Your Choices

  • No Account Required: You can play fully anonymously with no sign-in.
  • Ads: Ads only appear in Kudosu Mode. You can avoid ads entirely by not using Kudosu Mode.
  • Data Deletion: To delete your local game data, go to Settings > General > iPhone Storage > KaizenDoku > Delete App. For Sign in with Apple data, contact us at nathaniel.a.vazquez@gmail.com.

6. Tracking and Advertising Transparency

KaizenDoku complies with Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) requirements. When you first launch the app, you will be asked whether you want to allow KaizenDoku to track your activity across other companies' apps and websites for advertising purposes.

  • If you allow tracking: Google AdMob may use your device's IDFA (Identifier for Advertisers) to serve personalized ads relevant to your interests.
  • If you deny tracking: KaizenDoku will request only non-personalized ads. Your gameplay experience will not be affected.
  • Changing your choice: You can update your tracking preference at any time in Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

We do not use tracking data for any purpose other than serving ads through Google AdMob.

7. Children's Privacy

KaizenDoku is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn such data has been collected, we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The updated policy will be posted here with a revised "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: nathaniel.a.vazquez@gmail.com
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 24–48 hours.
