KaizenDoku Support

Welcome to the official support page for KaizenDoku, a Sudoku experience built around continuous improvement. This page provides guidance on using the app, troubleshooting common issues, and contacting our support team.

About KaizenDoku

KaizenDoku is a mobile Sudoku game for iPhone and iPad designed to help you grow as a puzzle solver. Key features include:

  • Classic Sudoku: Solve 9x9 puzzles across multiple difficulty levels, from beginner to expert.
  • Kudosu Mode: A unique ad-supported mode where watching optional ads unlocks additional puzzles and rewards.
  • Kaizen Game Over: Instead of a simple fail screen, KaizenDoku shows you exactly where you went wrong and how to improve — turning every mistake into a learning opportunity.
  • Cognitive Dashboard: Track your solving patterns, improvement over time, and puzzle performance metrics.
  • Board Analysis: Deep-dive into completed puzzles to understand your decision-making.
  • Local-First Storage: All your progress is stored securely on your device — no account required.
  • Sign in with Apple: Optional, for backing up progress across devices.

Getting Started

  1. Installation:
    • Download KaizenDoku from the App Store.
    • Requires iOS 15.0 or later.
  2. Account Setup:
    • Play immediately with no sign-in required — your progress is saved locally on your device.
    • Use Sign in with Apple for optional cross-device backup.
  3. Gameplay:
    • Select a difficulty from the main menu and start solving.
    • Use hints sparingly — KaizenDoku tracks your hint usage as part of your improvement journey.
    • After each puzzle, review the Kaizen breakdown to see where you can improve.

Troubleshooting

  • App Crashes or Freezes:
    • Ensure your device is updated to the latest iOS version.
    • Restart the app or your device.
    • Reinstall via Settings > General > iPhone Storage > KaizenDoku > Offload App.
  • Progress Not Saving:
    • KaizenDoku stores data locally on your device. Ensure you have sufficient storage space.
    • Do not delete app data without first backing up via Sign in with Apple.
  • Ads Not Loading in Kudosu Mode:
    • Ensure you have an active internet connection.
    • Ads may not load in all regions — try again later.
  • Sign in with Apple Issues:
    • Log out and back in via Settings > Sign in with Apple.
    • Ensure your Apple ID is active and in good standing.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • Is an account required to play?
    No. KaizenDoku is fully playable without an account. All progress is saved locally on your device.
  • What is Kudosu Mode?
    Kudosu Mode is a special puzzle mode supported by optional ads. Watch an ad to unlock a puzzle session — no purchase required.
  • Does KaizenDoku have in-app purchases?
    No. KaizenDoku is free to download and play. Kudosu Mode is supported by optional ad views.
  • What are the system requirements?
    KaizenDoku requires iOS 15.0 or later and supports iPhone and iPad.
  • How does the Kaizen Game Over screen work?
    When you complete or fail a puzzle, KaizenDoku analyzes your moves and highlights specific cells or patterns where improvement is possible, so each session makes you a better solver.
